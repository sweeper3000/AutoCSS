AutoCSS

Getting you started, fast.

What is it?

AutoCSS is a stylesheet that has a list of default styles so you can get a start on your project.

Functions

The things that AutoCSS does:

  • Fonts: Roboto & Roboto Slab are used; pulled from Google Fonts.
  • Headers: Use Roboto and are centered on the page.
  • Body Text: Uses Roboto Slab.
  • Print Styles: Body text 12pt, headers bolded.
